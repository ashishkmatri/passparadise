Title: Bella Bunny's Easter Egg Hunt

[SCENE: A springtime meadow with flowers blooming and butterflies flying]
Spring had arrived! Flowers bloomed everywhere and butterflies danced in the warm breeze.

[SCENE: A fluffy bunny named Bella hopping excitedly with a basket]
Little Bella Bunny was so excited! Today was the Easter Egg Hunt!

[SCENE: Bella looking at other bunnies who already have baskets full of colorful eggs]
But when she arrived at the meadow, other bunnies had already found so many eggs!

[SCENE: Bella searching behind flowers and under bushes, finding nothing]
Bella looked behind flowers and under bushes, but she couldn't find any eggs.

[SCENE: Bella sitting down looking sad, her basket still empty]
Her basket was empty. Bella sat down feeling sad. "I'm not good at finding eggs," she sniffled.

[SCENE: Bella noticing a tiny chick looking lost and scared near a bush]
Then she heard a small peep. A tiny chick was lost and looking for its mother!

[SCENE: Bella comforting the scared little chick, offering her paw]
Bella forgot about the eggs. "Don't worry little one, I'll help you find your mommy!"

[SCENE: Bella carrying the chick on her back, hopping through the meadow looking for mother hen]
She carried the chick on her back, hopping through the meadow searching everywhere.

[SCENE: Mother hen running toward them with joy, the chick chirping happily]
Finally, they found Mother Hen! "My baby!" she cried happily. The chick was so happy!

[SCENE: Mother Hen giving Bella a beautiful golden egg as a thank you]
"Thank you, kind bunny," said Mother Hen, giving Bella a beautiful golden egg!

[SCENE: Other bunnies gathering around Bella, impressed by her kindness]
The other bunnies gathered around. "You're a hero, Bella!" they said, sharing their eggs with her.

[SCENE: Bella hopping home with friends, her basket now full, everyone happy]
The end. Remember, helping others is the greatest treasure we can find!
