Title: Diya the Little Lamp

[SCENE: A beautiful Indian home decorated with flowers and rangoli patterns for Diwali]
It was the festival of Diwali, the happiest time of year! Homes were decorated with colors and joy.

[SCENE: A small clay lamp (diya) sitting on a shelf, looking at bigger, fancier lamps]
On a shelf sat a tiny clay lamp named Diya. She was small and plain next to the bigger, golden lamps.

[SCENE: The bigger lamps being placed in windows and doorways, shining brightly]
The beautiful big lamps were placed in every window. They shone so bright and proud!

[SCENE: Diya still on the shelf, feeling sad and forgotten]
Little Diya stayed on the shelf. "I'm too small to matter," she thought sadly.

[SCENE: A little girl noticing Diya on the shelf and picking her up gently]
But then, a little girl named Priya noticed her. "Look, Amma! This little lamp is so cute!"

[SCENE: Priya's grandmother smiling and showing Priya how to fill the lamp with oil]
Grandmother smiled. "The smallest lamp can hold the biggest light," she said, filling Diya with oil.

[SCENE: Priya carefully lighting Diya with a match, the flame catching beautifully]
Priya carefully lit Diya's wick. A beautiful golden flame danced to life!

[SCENE: Diya's small flame glowing warm and bright, making Priya smile]
Diya's flame glowed warm and steady. It wasn't the biggest, but it was full of love.

[SCENE: Priya placing Diya near the family prayer area, the most special spot]
Priya placed Diya in the most special spot - near the family prayers. Diya felt so proud!

[SCENE: The whole home glowing with lights, big and small, all equally beautiful]
That night, all the lamps glowed together. Big and small, each one made Diwali brighter.

[SCENE: The family gathered around, their faces glowing with the warm light of many diyas]
The family gathered, their faces glowing with love and light. Diya was the happiest of all!

[SCENE: Diya shining brightly with other lamps, spreading light in the darkness]
The end. Remember, every light matters. Even the smallest flame can chase away the darkness!
